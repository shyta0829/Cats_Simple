barn kitty program

"Barn cats" are feral, un-socialized, or free-roaming cats who cannot be placed in a home environment. Placing them as "barn cats" on rural property is a humane, practical alternative to euthanasia. Barn cats discourage rodents, provide companionship for horses and other farm animals, and can live out their lives in relative security and comfort. If you can provide shelter, and a regular supply of food and water, a barn cat may be ideal for you. Barn cats are FREE of charge (a municipal pet license may be required) and are spayed or neutered, vaccinated, micro-chipped, and treated for parasites. If you are interested in adopting a barn cat, or have questions, contact shytah@CatRescueandHospital.org.
